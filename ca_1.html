Famous Artworks

1. Mona Lisa

The Mona Lisa is a world-famous portrait painted by Leonardo da Vinci between 1503 and 1506. It depicts a woman with a mysterious smile, believed to be Lisa Gherardini. This masterpiece is displayed in the Louvre Museum, Paris, and is admired for its realism and enigmatic expression.

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci
Year: 1503-1506

2. The Starry Night

The Starry Night is a famous painting by *Vincent van Gogh*, created in 1889 while he was in an asylum in Saint-Rémy-de-Provence. It shows a swirling night sky over a quiet village, expressing intense emotion and imagination. Today, it is displayed in the *Museum of Modern Art (MoMA), New York*, and is one of the most recognized artworks in the world.

Artist: Vincent van Gogh
Year: 1889

3. The Persistence of Memory

The Persistence of Memory is a surrealist masterpiece painted by *Salvador Dalí* in 1931. It features melting clocks in a dreamlike landscape, symbolizing the fluidity of time. The artwork is housed in the *Museum of Modern Art (MoMA), New York*, and remains one of Dalí’s most iconic works.

Artist: Salvador Dalí
Year: 1931
